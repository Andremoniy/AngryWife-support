Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Angry Wife (“the app”) handles information. The app is designed to be simple and privacy-conscious. In general, your data stays on your device unless you choose to export it or contact support.

1. Information you enter into the app

The app may store information that you intentionally create, such as:

  • mood entries
  • selected reasons or notes
  • history used to display trends and predictions

This information is used only to provide the app’s features, such as mood history, pattern detection and next-day prediction.

2. Data storage

Unless explicitly stated otherwise inside the app, your data is stored locally on your device. The app does not require account creation for core functionality.

3. Internet connectivity

The core functionality of the app does not require an internet connection. If you choose to contact support by email or use external links, those actions may involve third-party services such as your email provider or web browser.

4. Data sharing

The app does not sell your personal data. The app does not share your mood history with the developer unless you explicitly choose to export data or send information to support yourself.

5. Analytics and tracking

By default, this policy assumes the app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking.

If you later add analytics, crash reporting, advertising or other third-party services, you must update this Privacy Policy and your App Store privacy disclosures accordingly.

6. Predictions and app content

Any predictions, trends or pattern insights shown by the app are generated from recorded in-app history and are provided for informational or entertainment purposes only. They are not medical, psychological or professional advice.

7. Exported data

If the app allows you to export data, reports or screenshots, the exported content is created at your direction. Once exported, that content may be handled by other apps or services you choose to use, such as Files, Mail or messaging apps.

8. Children’s privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age required by applicable App Store rules in your region. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

9. Data deletion

Because data is generally stored locally on your device, you can delete your in-app data by removing entries within the app, if that feature is available, or by deleting the app from your device.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version should be published on this page with a revised effective date.
